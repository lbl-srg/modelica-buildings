Buildings.Rooms.Constructions.BaseClasses.PartialConstruction

Partial model for exterior construction that has no window

Information

This is the base class that is used to model opaque constructions in the room model.

The surface azimuth is defined in Buildings.HeatTransfer.Types.Azimuth and the surface tilt is defined in Buildings.HeatTransfer.Types.Tilt
