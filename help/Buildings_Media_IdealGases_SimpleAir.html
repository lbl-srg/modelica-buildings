Buildings.Media.IdealGases.SimpleAir

Air: Simple dry air model (-50..100 degC)

Information

This package is identical to Modelica.Media.Air.SimpleAir except for the minimum fluid temperature. The package is here for convenience so that all medium models that are typically used with the Buildings library are at a central location.

Extends from Modelica.Media.Air.SimpleAir (Air: Simple dry air model (0..100 degC)).
